Account and Data Deletion

Last updated: 26 June 2026

This page explains how to delete your Ellyhelp account and the personal data we hold about you. This is provided to comply with the Google Play Data Safety requirements and the UK GDPR right to erasure.

What gets deleted

When you delete your Ellyhelp account, we permanently remove:

  • Your email address
  • Your encrypted password
  • Your blocked app list
  • Your focus session history
  • Your subscription record (after the legal retention period — see below)

How to delete your account

Option 1: From within the app

1. Open Ellyhelp 2. Go to Settings 3. Scroll to the bottom and tap Delete account 4. Confirm — your account and data are queued for deletion immediately

Option 2: Email us

If you cannot access the app or prefer to do it manually, email ellyhelpsupport@gmail.com from the email address associated with your account. Include the word "delete" in the subject line.

We will:

  • Verify it is your account (we may ask a confirmation question)
  • Confirm receipt within 5 working days
  • Complete deletion within 30 days

What we keep, and why

Some information is kept after account deletion because UK law requires it:

What How long Why
Subscription / payment records 6 years Required by HMRC for tax reporting
Anonymous error logs 90 days Used to debug app crashes, contain no personal data
Backup copies Up to 90 days Automatic system backups that overwrite themselves

After these retention periods, all information is permanently and irreversibly deleted.

If you have an active subscription

Deleting your account does not automatically cancel an active subscription. You must cancel separately:

  • Google Play subscription: Open the Google Play app → Profile icon → Payments & subscriptions → Subscriptions → Ellyhelp → Cancel

If you delete your account without cancelling, your subscription will continue to bill until you cancel it via the platform that processed the payment.

Questions

If you have any questions about account or data deletion, email ellyhelpsupport@gmail.com.
