Privacy Policy

Last updated: 26 June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Ellyhelp ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you use the Ellyhelp mobile application (the "Service").

We are based in England, United Kingdom, and we comply with the UK General Data Protection Regulation (UK GDPR) and the Data Protection Act 2018.

1. Who we are

Ellyhelp is a digital wellbeing application that helps people reduce screen time by allowing them to block selected apps during focus sessions.

Contact: ellyhelpsupport@gmail.com

For the purposes of UK GDPR, Ellyhelp is the data controller of any personal information you provide through the Service.

2. What information we collect

We collect only what we need to make the Service work. Specifically:

Information you give us directly

  • Email address — used to create your account, sign you in, and recover your password.
  • Password — stored only in encrypted form by our authentication provider (Supabase). We never see or store your password in plain text.
  • Subscription status — if you upgrade to Ellyhelp Premium, we store whether you have an active subscription and when it expires. We do not store your payment card details — these are handled entirely by Google Play.

Information collected automatically by the app

  • Blocked app list — the list of apps you have chosen to block. This is stored on your device and (if you are signed in) synced to your account so it works across devices. We do not share this list with anyone.
  • Focus session data — start and end times of your focus sessions, how long they were, and whether they completed. Stored against your account so you can see your history.
  • Screen time data — read from your device's Usage Access permission and shown to you in the app. This data stays on your device and is not uploaded to our servers.

Information we do NOT collect

  • We do not collect your name, address, phone number, or date of birth.
  • We do not track which specific apps you open or how long you spend in them outside of focus sessions.
  • We do not use any third-party analytics or advertising trackers.
  • We do not sell or share your data with anyone for marketing purposes.

3. Why we collect it

We process your personal data on the following legal bases under UK GDPR:

Data Why Legal basis
Email & password To create and secure your account Contract (Article 6(1)(b))
Subscription status To unlock premium features you've paid for Contract
Blocked app list & sessions To make the Service function as you've requested Contract
Service improvements To fix bugs and improve features Legitimate interest (Article 6(1)(f))

4. How we store and protect your information

  • Account data is stored on Supabase (servers located in the European Union).
  • All connections between the app and our servers use HTTPS / TLS encryption.
  • Passwords are hashed and salted; even we cannot read them.
  • Payment details are never stored by us — Google Play handles these directly under its own privacy policy.

5. How long we keep your information

  • Active accounts: for as long as your account exists.
  • Deleted accounts: when you delete your account, your data is removed from our active systems within 30 days. Backup copies may persist for up to 90 days before automatic deletion.
  • Subscription records: kept for 6 years after the last transaction, as required by UK tax law.

6. Your rights

Under UK GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Correct any inaccurate data
  • Delete your account and personal data ("right to erasure")
  • Export your data in a machine-readable format
  • Object to processing in certain circumstances
  • Withdraw consent at any time where consent is the legal basis
  • Lodge a complaint with the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO)

To exercise any of these rights, email us at ellyhelpsupport@gmail.com. We aim to respond within 30 days.

7. Children and young people

Ellyhelp is intended for users aged 13 and over. If you are under 13, please do not create an account. If we learn that we have collected personal information from a child under 13, we will delete it promptly.

Users aged 13 to 17 should have a parent or guardian's permission before creating an account, particularly before subscribing to Ellyhelp Premium.

8. Sharing your information

We share your information only with the following service providers, and only as needed to operate the Service:

  • Supabase (authentication and database) — provides our account system and stores user data.
  • Google Play Billing (Android subscription payments) — processes subscription payments if you subscribe through the app.

Each of these providers has their own privacy policy and acts as a "data processor" on our behalf. We do not allow them to use your data for their own marketing.

We do not share, sell, or rent your personal information to any other third parties.

We may disclose information if required by law (for example, in response to a court order from a UK court).

9. International transfers

Some of our service providers (e.g. Google) may transfer your data outside the UK or EU. Where this happens, we rely on Standard Contractual Clauses or equivalent safeguards to ensure your data remains protected.

10. Cookies and tracking

The mobile app does not use cookies, and we do not use marketing or analytics cookies.

11. Changes to this policy

If we make significant changes to this policy, we will notify you in the app and update the "Last updated" date at the top. Continued use of the Service after changes means you accept the updated policy.

12. Contact

Questions about this policy or your data:

Email: ellyhelpsupport@gmail.com

If you are not satisfied with our response, you can complain to the UK Information Commissioner's Office (ICO).
